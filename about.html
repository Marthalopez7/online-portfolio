About Me

The Grand Budapest Hotel

The Grand Budapest Hotel is a film directed by Wes Anderson released in 2014. This film is set in the Grand Budapest Hotel in the 1930’s. Originally, the hotel was very big and popular with a lot of people visiting and staying at the hotel. However, after the death of one of the guests mysteriously passes away and the hotel’s concierge Gustave H. receives a very expensive painting from her will and her family believes that he murdered her. In this murder-mystery type film, followed by Wes Anderson's amazing cinematics, viewers are entranced by the story. This is one of my favorite films and I will always recommend it to others.
