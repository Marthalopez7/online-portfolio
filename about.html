About Me

The Grand Budapest Hotel

The Grand Budapest Hotel is a film directed by Wes Anderson released in 2014. This film is set in the Grand Budapest Hotel in the 1930’s. Originally, the hotel was very big and popular with a lot of people visiting and staying at the hotel. However, after the death of one of the guests mysteriously passes away and the hotel’s concierge Gustave H. receives a very expensive painting from her will and her family believes that he murdered her. In this murder-mystery type film, followed by Wes Anderson's amazing cinematics, viewers are entranced by the story. This is one of my favorite films and I will always recommend it to others.

Dead Poets Society

Dead Poets Society is a film directed by Peter Weir in 1989. Starring legendary actor Robin Williams as professor John Keating. This film takes place at an all-boys preparatory school known for its successes and traditions. Keating uses new innovative techniques to educate his students in the curriculum as well as teaching his students important life lessons. The audience follows a group of friends who evolve and experience significant changes in their lives. Professor Keating helps these students grow and evolve by supporting them and providing advice when needed.

Portrait of a Lady on Fire

Portrait of a Lady on Fire is a French film directed by Céline Sciamma. Set in the eighteenth century on an island in Brittany, a young female painter is tasked with creating a wedding portrait of another young lady. However, she must do so without the young lady taking notice. Céline Sciamma really shows her skills as a director by showing the world through a woman's perspective using her personal experiences and insights as a woman. This film encaptures every emotion and really captivates the audience. In the future, I hope to share my perspective through my own work as well and hopefully have as great of an impact as the amazing Céline Sciamma.

Gia

Gia is a film that documents real-life Gia Carangi and her rise to fame as the world's first supermodal. Directed by Miceal Cristofer in 1998, his excellent use of cinematography and realness further invoked the realness and humanity of Gia Carangi. Gia starts off working at a diner when she meets a photographer and participates in a photoshoot. Shortly after, she learns that people think that she can be a star when she travels to New York City. Shortly after arriving, modal agent Wilhelmina Cooper immediately takes her in, seeing Gia’s potential. The film walks us through Gia’s experiences and does not leave out any hardships and pain. Which makes this film feel so human and real.

Depeche Mode

Depeche Mode is a rock-electronic band popular in the 80’s. This group consists of two members Dave Gahan and Martin Gore. However originally it consisted of more members including Andrew Fletcher, Alan Wilder, and Vince Clarke. Their style of music is mostly electronic but can range from rock to pop. I grew up listening to this band and it is definitely one of my favorites.

Maná

Maná is a Spanish rock band also popular in the 80’s. They are considered to be one of the best Latin American bands of all time, and this is not just specific to rock. Their songs were a necessity growing up and continue to be as they provide comfort as well as motivation. Waking up every Sunday or Saturday morning to my dad cleaning with Maná in the background would motivate me to clean as well.

Brenton Wood

Brenton Wood is an American singer and songwriter popular in the late 60s and 70s. He sings mostly soul songs and his rhythm is almost always very joyful. This is one of my recent discoveries in music graciously recommended by a friend. Brenton Wood’s music is perfect for anytime you want to listen to a different genre of music or just when you want to feel a little happy and add a bounce to your step.

Black Sabbath

Black Sabbath is a heavy metal rock band popular in the 70s. They are an English rock band from Birmingham. The group consists of Tony Lommi, Bill Ward, Geezer Butler, and Ozzy Osbourne. They are considered to be the pioneers of heavy metal. Their songs Black Sabbath, Paranoid, and Master of Reality are known for this.

The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a video game series that takes place during an apocalypse. There are currently two parts to this game and it has easily become my top favorite game. In the first game, we follow Joel as he encounters a young girl named Ellie who is immune to the bite of an infected. Together they set out to find the fireflies in hopes of finding a cure. This story has amazing visuals, gameplay, and music. However, my favorite part of the game is the beautiful story that unfolds as you play the game, with moments that you only wish you could play again for the first time.

Subnautica

Subnautica is an underwater game that takes place on an alien planet. After your spaceship crashes on an alien planet, you are forced to find a way to survive until you find a way off the planet. This game provides a mixture of suspense, horror, and fun. This game’s unique characters and storyline are my favorite aspects of the game.

Horizon Zero Dawn

Horizon Zero Dawn is the first part of a two part video game. The story takes place in the future after the current world is destroyed and humans revert back to a more tribal group. The story follows Aloy, an outcast, and her journey around the world trying to figure out her past. This game really took some concepts I had never seen before and blended them into one game. As of right now, I have not yet finished the second part of the game Horizon Forbidden West but based on where I am I do enjoy the first part (Horizon Zero Dawn) more.

Titanfall 2

Titanfall 2 is a first-person-shooter game that follows pilot Jack Cooper and titan BT. The game follows as the pair try to stop a catastrophic event from occurring using everything within their power. Facing unique bosses, their goal is to overcome them and stop the catastrophic event from occurring. This was my first fps game and I really enjoyed it so much, so it holds a very special place in my heart.

LunarHacks 2022

LunarHacks was a hackathon created by women for women. This women only hackathon was aimed at women who are underrepresented in the tech field and wanted to succeed in this field. This hackathon was open to beginners and people from around the world. In my experience, I networked with new people and joined their team for the hackathon. Consequently, they were all from Canada while I was not, so I learned some interesting things from them. In this hackathon you had two days to code a project using whichever programming language you preferred from the available topics.
