Writing Examples

DelveHomes Reflective Essay

My first written example is titled Career Research Essay. This essay was part of a longer assignment where we first conducted research. After researching different careers in tech, we were tasked with choosing one and conducting further research, specific to that job. In my case, I chose software development. In this essay, I described what software developers do, how they work, and who they work with. This essay also included how to build and start a career in software development. I selected this work to share because I believe it highlights my ability to research a topic and write an essay based on that research.

Career Research Essay

The second writing example is another essay titled Delve Homes Reflective Essay. This was an explanatory type of essay based on a web development project we had done in the past. In this essay, I described the details of the Delve Homes project and website. This means that I wrote an essay going into depth about the steps my partner and I took to complete the website and our challenges and successes. I chose this essay to share because I think it shows how I can write a reflective essay that is easy to understand and describes the project well.
